About

Analyze Your Dreams

dREaMEMBER is an app for you to analyze your dreams. Using definitions from the Dream Moods Dictionary, you can enter a word and receive an instant dream interpretation. This explanation will give you deeper insight into the significance of the symbols appearing in your dreams.

Record and Remember

Members can save dream dates, main themes and descriptions. This allows you to keep an ongoing diary of your dreams to review and analyze in the future. Through this continued self-investigation, you will gain a greater understanding of yourself and how your subconscious mind processes your daily life.
