About

Analyze

dREaMEMBER is an app for you to analyze your dreams. Using definitions from the Dream Moods Dictionary, you can enter a keyword and receive an instant interpretation. This explanation will give you deeper insight into your subconscious mind and what is happening in your life.

Record and Remember

Members can log their dreams with the date, main theme and a description. This allows you to keep an ongoing diary of your dreams for you to review and analyze in the future. Through this continued self-investigation, you will gain a greater understanding of how your mind processes your daily life.
